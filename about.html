About KAICON

Company Overview

Who We Are

KAICON is a diversified supply and services company specializing in the provision of industrial raw materials, semi-processed minerals, construction inputs, agribusiness commodities, logistics solutions, and auxiliary project support services across East Africa and beyond.

A Tanzanian company at heart, KAICON prioritizes local employment and skills transfer while maintaining global delivery standards. Our team of engineers, project managers, logistics experts, and skilled artisans delivers world-class outcomes on every engagement.
