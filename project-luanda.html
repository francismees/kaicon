Commercial

Luanda Commercial Tower

Mixed-use commercial development · Luanda, Angola

Project Overview

Representing KAICON's expanding footprint into Southern Africa, this project involved the core structural works and exterior glass cladding for an iconic commercial high-rise in Angola's capital.

The project required close coordination with international architectural firms and managing a bilingual workforce.

The Challenge

Navigating Angolan customs and cross-border logistics for specialized curtain-wall glass systems, alongside adapting to localized building codes and languages.

KAICON's Solution & Impact

We established a dedicated Angolan logistics entity to streamline imports. Our hybrid management team seamlessly bridged the language divide, delivering the striking glass facade safely and creating a new landmark on the Luanda skyline.
